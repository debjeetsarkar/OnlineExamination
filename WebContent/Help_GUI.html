    Welcome to the help section of online examination portal!



    This website is created for an online examination system. To appear` for this exam, please follow the instructions given below:


    1. Read the instructions carefully before attempting to give your exam.

    2. Click Sign Up in the home page to redirect to the registration page where you might be needed to provide some information.

    3. Fill out the registration form and click Register.

    4. You are now a registered member of this portal. Provide your user id and password in the homepage and click Sign In.

    6. Start your exam. Make sure that you check your time.

    7. Click the Submit button when your are finished.

    8.Get your score.
